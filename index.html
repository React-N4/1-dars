    About Data Entry/Book-keeper

    The Data Entry/Book-keeper will handle high volume data entry for the accounting department. This
    role is responsible for assisting with managing the company books and ensuring documentation is
    consistent and accurate. The successful candidate for this position will be a team player and a high
    performer

    Essential Duties and Responsibilities

    Enter or update accounting data into the company computer system

    Transfer data from paper documentation to electronic files and database systems

    Accurately and efficiently input direct source information as needed

    Create spreadsheets for balancing and accurately tracking figures

    Compile, verify, and sort information for review

    Check data for deficiencies or errors and correct any discrepancies

    Assist with organizing record-keeping of income and expenditures

    Input journal entries for all receipts, payments, and other financial transactions

    Assist with filing source documents for all journal entries recorded

    Performing other supportive bookkeeping and data entry duties as required

    Skills/Qualifications

    Knowledge with PCS software is a must

    Self-directed and detail oriented

    Advanced skills with Microsoft Office, especially Excel

    Ability to prioritize and meet deadlines with specified time constraints

    Strong verbal and written communication skills

    Good organizational skills

    At least 40 wpm and 10-key experience

    Team player and critical thinker

    Experience and/or Education Requirement

    2-4 years of experience bookkeeping, and administrative work

    QuickBooks & Excel experience strongly preferred

    Able to handle critical fist paced environment
