About the position for Logistics Coordinators

Trucking Times, Inc. is a trusted partner for our customers through outstanding service and commitment to excellence. Trucking Times, Inc. is an experienced company and the years we have grown into one of the most successful companies in transportation, combining third-party logistics, asset-based trucking, and advanced supply chain solutions into one complementary package. Today we have multiple locations across the United States, and we manage a network of 48 states regarding freight for our customers. As a logistics coordinator at TTI, you will be responsible for overseeing all in-transit shipments while working directly with carriers to move our customer’s freight in the most economic and efficient way possible. Our best coordinators are aggressive, energetic problem solvers who thrive in our fast paced work environment. If this sounds like you, we welcome you to apply today to become a successful member of TTI.

Responsibilities of a Logistics Coordinator

  • Track and trace shipment progress
  • Manage daily freight schedules
  • Develop and maintain relationships with new and existing carriers
  • Proactive communication with customer service team and sales reps

Qualities/Requirements

  • Strong multi-tasking skills
  • Excellent communication/organization skills
  • Positive attitude
  • High Energy
  • Strong work ethic
  • Assertive problem solving/decision making skills
  • Tough negotiating skills
