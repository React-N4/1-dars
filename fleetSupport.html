About Fleet Support Coordinator

As a Fleet Support Coordinator you learn how to make a difference as a Fleet Manager. We will train you on how to lead a fleet of professional over the road drivers and how to drive performance in the key business metrics of Safety, Service, Revenue, and Retention. You will work directly with our experienced Fleet Managers as you learn how to run a successful fleet of drivers. As a crucial part of your learning you will provide support by completing status checks on drivers and communicate any ETA changes, read and respond to driver messages, coordinating certain safety tasks and coordinating maintenance events. Once you have mastered this position you will be promoted to a Fleet Manager and will manage your own fleet of 35-45 drivers. This is incredibly rewarding work that will provide you with a high level of personal and professional satisfaction.

You create Driver Success by

Seeing your drivers as unique and special individuals who are separate from the equipment that they operate

Meeting their personal needs by using the Key Principles in your interactions

Meeting their practical needs by supporting their Fleet Manager's operational leadership

Respecting their time by making sure their time at work is effectively utilized every day

Essential Duties and Responsibilities

At Trucking Times, Inc. we value Mutual Support & Teamwork, Respect, Making a Difference, Honesty, Integrity, Accountability, Customer Care, Driver Success, and our Cornerstone Value: Safety! These values guide our daily interactions with each other and our customers. We are looking for an individual who shares our values, someone who is looking to grow and develop professionally with a team who is committed to their success! We work hard and we have fun collaborating together as we accomplish our goals! Our industry is very dynamic and no two days are the same. That is one of the reasons we love what we do! So you will need to be very adaptable, able to plan, organize, prioritize, and complete multiple tasks. Since you are working closely with a wide group of people it is critical that you are able to effectively empathize, be a solid communicator, a first rate listener, and possess a high level of self-awareness.
